# Anaphylaxis

Anaphylaxis em wanwan alaji riyakshan weya imin kilim pipul. Lajim det pipul la daun, gibim adrenalin (EpiPen) raitwei nomo, kolim 000, en gibim sekund wan afta 5 minit sipos yunu luk imin statim beta.

## Lukim Anaphylaxis

Eni **wanwan** la deting afta det pipul bin katch an alajik, emin Anaphylaxis:
- Hardfella or noizi wun la brit
- Tang swellin
- Trot swellin or tait
- Hardfella la tok en/or noizi vais
- Wiyul or kof weya imin goon goon
- Dizi or foldan weya imin goon goon
- Yelafella en sof (lilirawan kid)

Lilirawan sain main inkludim la skin, lip and ai swellin, beli pein or wamait. Deting main kwikfella kom bigfella tumas.

## Walat yu main duu

1. **Lajim det pipul la daun.** No letim det pipul stan or wokin – deting main mekim det pipul dai raitwei nomo. Sipos imin hardfella la brigin, letim imin sidon en stredim leg aot, bat nomo mekim imin stan.
2. **Gibim adrenalin** wid wanwan autoinjikta (EpiPen) la aotsaid mitul la thai – thurru klobin em ok. Holdim la **3 sekon**.
3. **Kolim 000.**
4. Afta 5 minit, sipos yunu luk imin statim beta, **gibim sekund wan dosis** sipos yu gulum.
5. Sipos det pipul imin ketch imin no riak, en imin no britin gud, statim [CPR](/kb/cpr).
6. Sipos imin gulum astma sain tu, gibim adrenalin foswan, den wanwan [blu astma rilivur](/kb/astma).

## Yuzum EpiPen

1. Meikim han laiki wanwan fist garrat EpiPen – **blu la skai, orinj la thai.**
2. Pulim op det blu sevti kep.
3. Putum det orinj en la aotsaid mitul la thai en pushim thurru wanwan klik.
4. Holdim la 3 sekon.
5. Mabim det ples la 10 sekon.
6. Raidim det taim so yu main sabi wen la gibim det sekund wan dosis.
