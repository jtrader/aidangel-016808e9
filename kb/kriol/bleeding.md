# Bala Blud Ibin Kam Owt Foa-Foa

Stopim bala blud ibin kam owt foa-foa tru long yu putum hand strong-strong, direk, kontinyuwus ontop long wun, putum det pesin daun, en helpim foa shock wile yu waintaim ambulens.

## Statim miting dumaj

1. **Kollim 000** foa bala blud ibin kam owt foa-foa.
2. Folowim [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
3. Putum det pesin daun en soponim det wun (katum klos sapos miit).
4. Putum **hand yu stong-stong, direk, kontinyuwus** witim yu hand, wan klin klot o wan pad.
5. Wan’gen blud ibin opm, holim det presha de witim wan stong banij.
6. Holim det presha de — yu **nogat** liftimap det drasing foa luk de.
7. Helpim foa [shock](/kb/shock) en kipim det pesin wom.

## Ting Ibin Stat De

Yu **nogat** tekim owt wan ting ibin stat long wan wun. Putum presha long evri sait long det ting en bilidimap pad raun de foa stopim im muwum bifoa yu banijim.

## Amputation

- Opmim blud witim direkt presha.
- Rapum det pat ibin katim long wan klin drai klot, putum de long wan plastik bag, den putum det bag long ais – **noba agen long ais srait o long wota**.
- Sendim de long hospitul witim det pesin.

## Tourniquet

Wan komashal tourniquet mas bi yus oni foa wun long lek o han ibin blid longwei en yu ken stopim witim direkt presha. Putum 5–7 cm antap long det wun (noba antapwan jain), taitum im til blud hemin stop en raitim daun taim.
