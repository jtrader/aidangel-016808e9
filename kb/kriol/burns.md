# Bungun imin hot langa skin (Burns and Scalds)

Kulumtji enijing weya bin bungun imin hot langa skin garra kol wata fo minimam 20 minit, tekum-out klos en jelri longdeiya if nomo teik-of, en kaba blanga samting klosi weya en nomo teik-out or samting klin en nomo teik-aut longdeiya.

## Titimin eni bungun olsem wunpat faswan

1. **Stopim datfala bungun.** Gowe longdeiya weya imin hot. If klos imin hot: stop, drop, kaba, rol.
2. Foloum [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd) if datfala pipul imin sikwan.
3. **Kulumtji datfala bungun** garra **kol wata fo minimam 20 minit**. Diswan imin go insaid 3 awa afta datfala bungun.
4. Tekum-out klos en jelri weya imin taitwan longdeiya — libum enijing weya imin teik-out langa skin.
5. Kaba blanga samting klosi weya en nomo teik-out or **cling wrap** weya imin leidan flat (nomo kaba taitwan raunwan langa wanpa leg).
6. Titimin fo [shock](/kb/shock) if datfala bungun imin bigwan.

## Wen fo kol 000

- Bungun imin bigwan pasin wanpa 20-sen koin.
- Bungun langa fes, hed, fut, jenitl ensaid or ovakan jain.
- Bungun langa pikinini, oldmaun pipul, or eniwan garra neda siknes.
- Elektrisiti or kemikol bungun.
- Inheleshan bungun (smok, stim).

## Nomof meikim diswan

- **Nomof** putum loshan, ointmen, bata, tutpeis or ais.
- **Nomof** blasta blistawen.
- **Nomof** pulum-of klos weya imin teik-aut.

## Kemikol bungun

Brushim-of drai kemikol faswan, den washim garra kol wata fo minimam 20 minit. Werim glaves fo prottek yuselp.

## Elektrisiti bungun

Turnim-of datfala pawo sos faswan. Datfala bungun imin luk smolwan bat imin meikim dip inaid imej — oltaim lukum medikal asessment.
