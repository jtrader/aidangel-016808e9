# Hipotamia (Hypothermia)

Hipotamia em detfala taim we binis blong bodi i drop daon long 35°C. Move-im daon detfala pepul long seilta, teik-im of ol wetpala klos, wam-im-ap slo-slo long blankit end bodi hit, end kol-im 000 sipos i strong bikaus i nogud.

## Sain

- Trigla (mait stap long strongpala keis)
- Kol, paelpala skin
- Nomnes (numbness)
- Stokpala tok, konfyushen, droos (drowsiness)
- Slo end wikpala palmpalam end briting

## Wanem blong duim

1. **Kol-im 000** sipos i strong bikaus i nogud.
2. Move-im daon long wampala, drai, end seiltapla ples.
3. Handelim slo-slo — rongpala muvment kaan mekim hat stop wok.
4. Teik-im of wetpala klos; put-um ol drai leiya or blankit insted.
5. Kapa-im het end nek.
6. Wam-im-ap **slo-slo** long blankit, bodi wamnes end wampala (bat nomo hotpala) invaironmen.
7. Giv-im **wampala switpala dring** nomo sipos i puli non-ap (conscious). **Nomo alkaol or kafiin.**
8. Nomo rab-im skin or put-um dairek hit (hotpala wota botul, hita).
9. Ready blong stat-im [CPR](/kb/cpr) — Kintinyu-im longga daen normal long hipotamia.
