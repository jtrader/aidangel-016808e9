# Jellyfish en Langka Bilong Solwara Ol I Stingim Yu

Sapos yu stap long ples olsem Bundaberg narafala hap not, en samfala jellyfish ol i stingim yu, yu mas putum vinegar long sting bilong 30 sekon, bihain yu kolim 000. Sapos bluebottle o narafala jellyfish bilong mipla ples ya, tekemaut ol 'tentacles' en putum long wata ia i hat.

## Jellyfish Bilong Ples Hat (box jellyfish, Irukandji)

Eniwe not long Bundaberg, eni jellyfish sting yu mas lukim olsem hem samting ya mipla save dai long em.

1. **Kolim 000.**
2. Pour **vinegar** plentia long sting bilong yu long 30 sekon o moa.
3. Sapos nogat vinegar, yu mas tekemaut ol 'tentacles' bilong em (ol mainja bilong em i no save stingim yu sapos yumi putum gelv long han; mainja bilong yu silip i orait long short taim).
4. Yu mas redi long statim [CPR](/kb/cpr) — box jellyfish stingim mainja i save mekim hat bilong yumi stop.

## Bluebottle en narafala jellyfish i no bilong ples hat

1. Wasim hap ya long wata bilong solwara (naut fres wata) long tekemaut ol 'tentacles'.
2. Yu **no** yusum vinegar.
3. Putum hap ya long **hat wata** (naut hat tumas we yu no save bin) bilong 20 minit. Yu save mekim hat shewaw wok.
4. Sapos nogat hat wata, yu save yusum kol pak long pen.

## Stingray, stonefish, cone shell

Putum am o leg long **hat wata** long 90 minit. Kolim **000** — ol sting mainja bilong ol ya i save pulim daith.

## Blue-ringed octopus

Yu mas presam hap ya strong (olsem mainja bilong [snake bite](/kb/snake-bite)) en kolim **000**. Yu mas redi long CPR — poison bilong em i save mekim brithing masel bilong yu stop.
