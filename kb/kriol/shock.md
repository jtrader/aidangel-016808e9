# Shock (Blood i bin lusim nogud / Blood i nogo flow gud)

Shock em iwan dेंजरस kain sik wea blood i nogo flow gud long body. Sain blanga shock em i: skin blanga oltaim i kalt, na i drai, na i hevi, pulse i wik na i kwikwan, siklongbel na had long tingting. Lajim daon dispela pikinini, mekim gud dispela samting wea mekim engo shock, kipim em i wom na kolim 000.

## Luksave Shock

- Skin blanga oltaim i kalt, na i drai, na i hevi
- Pulse i kwikwan, na i wik
- Breath i kwikwan na i nogud
- Siklongbel, torst, dizzyness, had long tingting
- Had long silip, o long tingting i nogud
- Laswan em i kras olgeta na nogo savi tingting

## Yu mas mekim wanem

1. **Kolim 000.**
2. Bihevim [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
3. Lajim daon dispela pikinini — yu mas **nogo** raisim lek blanga (AFA5 i tok olsem).
4. Meikim samting wea mekim engo shock (fo example, stopim [blood blo blo](/kb/bleeding), meikim gud [bon](/kb/burns) o [anaphylaxis](/kb/anaphylaxis)).
5. Lusim klos wea i tight.
6. Kipim em i wom long blanket — yu mas **nogo** putim het stret long body.
7. Givim liklik wata oredi if em i fulli conscious, savi swolam, na no gat eni sik long bel.
8. If em i unconscious o had long breath, putim em insait long [recovery position](/kb/recovery-position).
9. Tokim em gut na stap wetim em longtaim bifo help i kam.
