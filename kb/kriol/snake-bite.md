# Neik Baid — Bandijim Strongim Blood Blaik Imobailaisayshan

Sapos yu tingim neik baid insaid Ostrelia, kulum 000 en putum wanpela bandijim strongim blood blaik imobailaisayshan fram we neik baidim ijo go antap long hol fut o han, den spilitim en kipim pesin stop olgeta.

## Wanem yu garra mekim (tingim ije poisonwan)

1. **Kulum 000.**
2. Biheivim [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
3. Kipim dija pesin **stop olgeta** en laidaun. Muwimap garra mekim dija poison go olgeta hap bodijinga.
4. Putum wanpela **bandijim strongim blood blaik imobailaisayshan**:
   - Yu yusum wanpela longfela, hevi-banij (o klos we yu tiorimap sapos yu nidim).
   - Statim ova dija baid sait, bandijim strong - jas olsem yu bandijim sapos yu sprainim.
   - Kipim go **antap long hol fut o han**, kavamap olsem masta yu ken.
   - Maakim dija baid sait long ausaid dija bandij.
5. **Spilitim** dija fut o han blo stopim eni muwimap.
6. Raidim taim neik baidim en taim yu putum bandij.
7. Tok gut en lukautim. Redi blo statim [CPR](/kb/cpr).

## No mekim dyswan

- Yu no garra **bosim** dija baid - poison we ije long skin garra helpim blo faindimaut huwan neik datwan.
- Yu no garra **katim** o **suckim** dija wound.
- Yu no garra putum wanpela **tuniket**.
- Yu no garra **pulumaot** dija bandij - onli paramediks o hospital staaf mekim dyswan.
- Yu no garra traim blo kechim o killim dija neik.
