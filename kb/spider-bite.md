# Spider Bites

Funnel-web and mouse spider bites are treated with pressure immobilisation like a snake bite. Redback bites are treated with an ice pack — do not bandage. Seek medical aid for any concerning symptoms.

## Funnel-web or mouse spider

Treat as life-threatening — apply **pressure immobilisation** (see [snake bite](/kb/snake-bite)) and call **000**.

## Redback spider

1. Apply a cold pack (wrapped) to relieve pain.
2. Do **not** apply a pressure bandage — it makes the pain worse.
3. Seek medical aid. Antivenom is available if needed.

## White-tail, huntsman, other Australian spiders

1. Wash the bite with soap and water.
2. Apply an ice pack.
3. Seek medical aid if symptoms worsen, the bite becomes infected, or the bite is on the face or near the eyes.
