# Sunburn

Move out of the sun, cool the area with cool water for 20 minutes, apply after-sun gel, drink plenty of water, and never burst blisters. Seek medical aid for severe burns or blistering on children.

## What to do

1. Move out of the sun.
2. Cool the affected skin under **cool running water for 20 minutes**.
3. Apply after-sun gel, aloe vera or a moisturising lotion.
4. Drink plenty of water to rehydrate.
5. Cover up before going back outside.
6. **Do NOT burst blisters.**

## Seek medical aid if

- The burn covers a large area
- There are extensive blisters
- The person is a baby, young child or elderly
- They have signs of [heat illness](/kb/heat-illness) — high temperature, vomiting, dizziness, confusion
