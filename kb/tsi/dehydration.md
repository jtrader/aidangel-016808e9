# Bodi drai

Muvum da pipul i go insaid long wan kol ples, an mekem da pipul i drang evri lelebet wata o drai bodi wata (oral rehydration solution) slo-slo. Sika drai bodi (severe dehydration) — haed i giris, lelebet im wata (urine) i stat stop, hat i bit i go kwik-kwik (rapid pulse) — nida kolam ambulans.

## Sain

- Nanggel, maot i drai
- Im wata (urine) i karanim kala, o nomo wata i pas
- Haed i hevi, haed i giris, bodi i taiad
- Ai i ron insaid, skin i drai
- Hat i bit i go kwik-kwik, an im brith i go kwik-kwik (sika drai bodi)

## Wanim yu garra du

1. Muvum i go insaid long wan kol ples, an mekem im i lagaot.
2. Drang wata o drai bodi wata (oral rehydration solution) evri lelebet slo-slo — drang evri lelebet, i bata long drang wanwan laif.
3. Nomu drang kofii, alklhol an miti dring.
4. Smola drai bodi (mild dehydration) long pikinini, giwam drai bodi wata (oral rehydration solution) (e.g. Hydralyte).
5. **Kolum 000** sapos da pipul i haed i giris, o im wata (urine) i stop pas, o im hat i bit i go kowik-kwik (rapid pulse), o im i slip (unconscious).
