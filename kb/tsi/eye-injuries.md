# Ai Bin Dat

Saposi ami garram blo yu ai, woshemot long klin wata o salt wata. Neva pulumot eni samting weh him bin go insaid — jast kavam blo yu ai tupela en kol 000. Saposi kemikal bin splash pas, yu mas putumdaon blo yu ai anjala wata go kas long 20 minit en den kol ambyulans.

## Samting insaid ai (sen, ailesh)

1. Nomata rubum blo yu ai.
2. Tiltim blo yu hed so dat det ai weh him bin dat, him mas bi daon.
3. Woshem long klin wata, salt wata o ai-wos solyusen.
4. Saposi him nomata kamanot, yu mas kavam blo yu ai long wan klin pat en den go luk medikal help.

## Samting weh bin go insaid

1. **Nomata** traiim pulumot.
2. Kavam blo yu ai luusli long wan peipa kap o pat — nomata presha.
3. Kavam blo yu **nara ai tu** fo stopim tupela ai muvin.
4. **Kol 000.**

## Kemikal Splash

1. **Straitemdaon** woshem blo yu ai long kuul daonranin wata fo gamin **20 minit**. Tiltim blo yu hed so dat det kemikal him mas ranwei fram det ai weh him nomata dat.
2. Hobulim det aidlid open.
3. **Kol 000.**
4. Saposi yu save det kemikal, tekandaon det konteina wit yu go long hospitul.

## Blow long Ai

Putum wan kool pak fo reduusim det swelin. Go luk medikal help saposi ami eni chenj long vision, strong pen, blad insaid det ai, o det ai luk abnormul.
