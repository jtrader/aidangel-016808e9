# Sanban

Go wea san shain nomoa, putum daun kuulkul wata f'20 minit, rubum gell daat yu yus afta san, dringk heapa wata, eno neva bastum blista. Luk f'healp f'dokta or kaman medicine f'strong sanban or blista on pikinini.

## Wanim yu garra du

1. Go wea san shain nomoa.
2. Putum daun kuulkul wata f'strong sanban **f'20 minit**.
3. Rubum gell daat yu yus afta san, alo vera or wan loshin daat mekum skin soft.
4. Dringk heapa wata f'fillapim gen bodi wata.
5. Kapa-ap bifo yu go bak autsaid.
6. **Eno bastum blista.**

## Luk f'healp f'dokta or kaman medicine if

- Sanban kapa heapa eria
- Blista heapa pas wea yu tingk
- Person emi wan beibi, yang pikinini or elda pesin
- Dem garra sain f'[hiit siknis](/kb/heat-illness) — hai tempra, vomitin, hedi go raun, yumi faind daat pesin konfyuus
