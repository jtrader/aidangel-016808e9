# Maḻŋ'marrma' rrupiya bäy yindiku 'yindi' yindi'mirri

Ga maḻŋ'marrma' rrupiya bäy yindiku 'yindi' yindi'mirri — gaŋga'yun, ngurrungu'yun ŋäḻindiŋu ŋäḻindiŋu, wirrŋ'thun — liŋgu yäku'yun marrtji, ga nhäŋiniŋa ŋuli bany'yandja ŋayi. Bili bany'yandja dhuwala biyapul, dhuwala yindiku'mirri rrupiya ŋuli biyapul. Ga nhäŋiniŋa dhuwala biyapul.

## Maḻŋ'marrma' rrupiya bäy yindiku 'yindi' yindi'mirri

- Ngurrungu'yun ŋäḻindiŋu, ŋäḻindiŋu bäy yindi
- Gaŋga'yun (birrka'yun rrupiya bäy yindi) bäy yindi
- Wirrŋ'thun bitjanu' ŋululu' ŋululu'mirri
- Wataŋu'yun, djudjukthurr (ŋurruŋu bäy yindi)

## Nhä nhe dhu ga nhina

1. Ga djämany'thun dhuwala nhini bäy yindiku 'yindi' yindi'mirri, ga nhina ga nhe dhu bany'yandja mayali'.
2. Ga nhina ŋululu' ŋululu'mirri, ga nhina dhuwala bäy yindi dhuwala [anaphylaxis](/kb/anaphylaxis).
3. Ga nhina dhuwala bäy yindi dhuwala biyapul, dhuwala yindiku'mirri rrupiya ŋuli biyapul.
4. Ga nhina dhuwala bäy yindi dhuwala biyapul, dhuwala yindiku'mirri rrupiya ŋuli biyapul.
5. **Gorru 000 yindiku'mirri** bili dhuwala yindiku'mirri rrupiya ŋuli biyapul, dhuwala yindiku'mirri rrupiya ŋuli biyapul. Ga nhina dhuwala biyapul, dhuwala yindiku'mirri rrupiya ŋuli biyapul. Ga nhina dhuwala biyapul, dhuwala yindiku'mirri rrupiya ŋuli biyapul. Ga nhina dhuwala biyapul, dhuwala yindiku'mirri rrupiya ŋuli biyapul.

Ga ŋuwala' ŋuli biyapul dhuwala yindiku'mirri rrupiya ŋuli biyapul, ga nhina dhuwala **adrenaline autoinjector ŋuli biyapul**, dhuwala yindiku'mirri rrupiya ŋuli biyapul.
