# Anaphylaxis

Anaphylaxis ga dhuḻuluṉa wäŋa-djäma yindinydja ŋulaŋuru maḻŋ'thurruna. Bukmak yana nhe galki'yirri wäŋalili, ga gaŋga galki'yirri EpiPen-nhana, ga guyaŋi 000-nhana, ga gi ŋuriŋi nhiniŋi’yirrina maḻŋ'thunna yindi ga 5 minitjtja wäŋa'yirri.

## Anaphylaxis-wuŋu dharaŋan

Gäthura **goḏirrina** ŋulaŋuru ŋupanana ŋulaŋuru yindi Anaphylaxis:
- Miṉḏi dhä-waḻa ga ŋarakalaḻi dhä-waḻana
- Goŋalili-dharray ga dhä-waḻana
- Goŋalili-dharray ga dhä-waḻana ga rurru-waḻana ga dhä-waḻana
- Dhä-waḻana ga dhä-rurruna ga dhä-waḻana yiindina
- Dhä-waḻana ga dhä-t̪urrana ga dhä-waḻana dhuwurr-djäl ga dhä-waḻana
- Dhä-waḻana ga dhä-wurruṉiyana ga dhä-waḻana ga dhä-gayaŋana ga dhä-waḻana
- Dhä-waḻana ga dhä-dhaluna ga dhä-waḻana (gurruṯuruŋu nhiniŋi’yirrina)

Nhiniŋi’yirrina rurru-djäl ga dhä-waḻana dhuwurr-djäl ga rurru-waḻana ga dhä-waḻana rurru-djäl ga galki'yirrina. Maḻŋ'thunna yindina.

## Marŋgithirrina

1. **Galki'yirri wäŋalili.** Yaka gi buku-waŋana — dhuwalana yindikana nhiniŋi’yirri. Yindi dhä-waḻana rurru-djäl ga dhä-waḻana, gi gurruṯuruŋu nhina, ga yaka gi buku-waŋana.
2. **Gi EpiPen-nhana dhuwalana** wuṉḏaŋarr-djäl ga dhä-waḻana rurru-djäl ga dhä-waḻana - djäga ga dhä-waḻana ga dhä-waḻana. Ga dhuwalana **3 sekon’tjtja**.
3. **Guyaŋi 000-nhana.**
4. 5 minitjtja wäŋa'yirri ga yaka gi nhiniŋi’yirri, **gi ŋuriŋi nhiniŋi’yirrina** yindi ga dhä-waḻana.
5. Yindi yaka gi nhiniŋi’yirri ga yaka gi dhä-waḻana, djurra [CPR](/kb/cpr)-nhana.
6. Yindi gi Asthma-wuŋu djäpana, gi EpiPen-nhana dhuwalana, ga dhuwalana [blue asthma reliever](/kb/asthma)-nhana.

## EpiPen-wuŋu djäpana

1. Ga dhä-waḻana EpiPen-nhana - **blue to the sky, orange to the thigh.**
2. Ga dhä-waḻana blue safety cap-nhana.
3. Ga dhä-waḻana orange end-nhana dhuwalana ga dhä-waḻana ga dhä-waḻana.
4. Ga dhuwalana 3 sekon’tjtja.
5. Ga dhä-waḻana dhä-waḻana 10 sekon’tjtja.
6. Ga dhä-waḻana dhä-waḻana dhuwalana yindi ga dhä-waḻana ga dhä-waḻana.
