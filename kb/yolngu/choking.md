# Gaarrwarra'wuy — Djarraŋul, Waŋupu, ga Yiki

Yanapi rom yaka nhina dhu gaarrwarra'wuynha, yaka ga dhu ga'yu, mala yiki, rrambaŋi dhu ga'yu 5-nhana dharrwa wiripunydja gaarrwarra'wunha warrpam' nhakun dhu nhanukalaŋuwuy nhina baṉarra’ ga balayi’nha dhawu' dhu ga'yu 5-nhana warrpam' gärakŋur ga balayi’na gaarrwarra'wuyaŋura nhanuŋu yiki dhu nhina dhulŋuŋu mala dhu nhina walŋamiriw.

## Gaarrwarra'wuya wurikiya

- **Birrka'mirri (dhuwali gaarrwarra'wuya):** romdhu ga dhu gaarrwarra'wuya, ga'yu mala dhu ga yiki. Dhuwaliya rom malana dhu gaarrwarra'wuya yanapi nhuŋu rrambaŋi gaarrwarra'wuya.
- **Wawa (yaka ga gaarrwarra'wuya):** yaka ga gaarrwarra'wuya, yaka ga ga'yu mala dhu ga yiki, ga dhu dhukarrnha dhiŋga'wuya dhuwali gaarrwarra'wuynha, ga dhu nhina gurrwi'y gaarrwarra'wuynha, ga dhu nhina mulmuya yanapi dhu wali walŋamiriw. Dhuwaliya rom malana dhu ga yaŋu ga wala gaarrwarra'wuya.

## Djarraŋul mala Waŋupu (balayi’na 1-ŋa wäyini)

1. **Buŋgawa 000** (mala rom dhu wali). 
2. Dhirra' nhanukalaŋuwuy gaarrwarra'wuya walatjpalŋur mala dhu ga'yu 5-nhana dhurrŋuri' wiripu dhuwalayi mala gaarrwarra'wuya waŋawuya mala dhu raŋinydja mala dhu dhuwali warrpam' mala gaarrwarra'wuya.
3. Yanapi gaarrwarra'wuynha yaka ga dhayu'mala dhu waliya, dhu ga'yu 5-nhana dhurrŋuri' wiripu dhuwali mala gaarrwarra'wuya waŋawuya mala dhu raŋinydja mala dhu dhuwali warrpam' mala gaarrwarra'wuya mala dhu waliy gaarrwarra'wuy.
4. **Maŋutji** 5-nhana dharrwa wiripunydja gaarrwarra'wunha mala dhu waliya, ga 5-nhana gaarrwarra'wuya wiripunydja gaarrwarra'wunha mala dhu waliya.
5. Yanapi dhu wali **walŋamiriw**, dhu waliya dhurrkap waŋawuya mala dhu bali waliya, ga dhu waliya CPR.

## Yiki (balayi’na 1-ŋa wäyini)

1. **Buŋgawa 000.**
2. Dhirra' nhanukalaŋuwuy gaarrwarra'wuya ŋathaŋur mala dhu waliya, ga dhu waliya dhurrkap waŋawuya mala dhu bali waliya dhuwaliya, ga dhu waliya warrpam' mala dhu waliya, ga dhu waliya walŋamiriw.
3. Dhuwaliya rom malana dhu ga'yu 5-nhana dhurrŋuri' wiripunydja gaarrwarra'wunha mala dhu waliya, ga dhu waliya warrpam' mala dhu waliya.
4. Dhuwaliya rom malana dhu ga dhurrŋuri' wiripunydja gaarrwarra'wunha mala dhu waliya, ga dhu waliya warrpam' mala dhu waliya, ga dhu waliya walŋamiriw.
5. Maŋutji dhu rrambaŋi dhu dhulŋuŋu gaarrwarra'wuynha mala dhu waliya, waŋawuya walŋamiriw — mala dhu waliya CPR.

## Yaka dhu

- Yaka dhu walŋamiriw mala dhu waliya gaarrwarra'wuy mala dhu waliya, ga dhu waliya dhurringiy, mala dhu waliya dhurrŋuri' wiripu.
- Yaka dhu waliya gaarrwarra'wuy mala dhu waliya (Heimlich) — Australian Resuscitation Council nhanuŋu mala dhu waliya gaarrwarra'wuy mala dhu waliya dhurrŋuri' wiripu ga gaarrwarra'wuy mala dhu waliya dhurrŋuri' wiripu.
