## Nhäyuŋuna?

1. **Wätharranana 000.** Yaka lakarama yolŋu yurru dharpina ga dharaŋginana warrarraŋina.
2. Gaŋgathinana yolŋunhana yurru nhinaŋu ga malthunana, goŋ warrarraŋina.
3. Yirraŋu ga dharaŋginana ga gurruṯumŋura.
4. aspirin **300 mg** (yurrkuŋuna ga ŋarraŋarrana walala dharpina ga dharaŋginana, 4 x 75–100 mg) yurru yakanha ŋarrakala dharpina ga dharaŋginana, walala yaka waṉa wurruŋinana, walala yaka waṉa aspirin.
5. Angina medication (GTN spray or tablets) walala yurru dharpina ga dharaŋginana.
6. Watharrana ga dharaŋginana, ga ŋarraŋarrana walala yurru dälkuŋuna.
7. Yaka walŋamirri, yaka djämamirri, yaka watha, [CPR](/kb/cpr) ga [AED](/kb/aed) yurru warrarrana.

## Djälk gaŋgathirri vs cardiac arrest

A **heart attack** dhuwalatj dharaŋgi ga ŋaḻindawuy walal dhambali ga djudjudj — yolŋu yurru walŋamirri. A **cardiac arrest** dhuwalatj djälk gurruṯum — yolŋu yurru dharaŋginana ga yaka djämamirri. Cardiac arrest yurru warrarrana CPR ga AED.
